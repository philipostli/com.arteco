Keep your plants healthy with real-time insights from the Arteco ZS-301Z soil sensor. The compact Zigbee probe measures soil moisture, soil fertility, air temperature, humidity, and light level directly at the root zone, giving you everything you need to water and feed your plants at exactly the right time.

Set up automatic alerts when the soil gets too dry, fine-tune readings with built-in calibration offsets, and adjust how often the sensor samples. All data is stored as history in Homey so you can spot trends and understand exactly what your plants need.
